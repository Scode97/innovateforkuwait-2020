About us

Welcome to 2020

We are a sustainable, biodegradable, renewable, and utterly eco-friendly company offering an alternative solution for plastic usage in Kuwait, aiming for a better future starting within the region, and further spreading across the GCC. Our target is to replace the one-time use of plastic, given the negative impact and endless harm it holds towards our environment, using the world’s fastest growing plant, bamboo!

Shop now

Mission

A sustainable environment aimed to cater B2B and B2C businesses through the provision of a purely natural alternative for plastic. ​

Vision

Vastly thriving business within Kuwait and the GCC due to the expected gradual growth in the usage of bamboo for the making of multiple products, namely, wrappers, clothes, sheets, papers, parchments, and most importantly, for a pure contribution to existing B2B and B2C businesses.
